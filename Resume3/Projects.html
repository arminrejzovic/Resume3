My Past Projects

There are many projects I have worked on and completed during the years. Most of them have been for personal use. I will list some of them below.

Android Development

eMatura - Maturski ispit za gimnazije

eMatura - Maturski ispit za gimnazije is my most successful project. It is an educational app meant to help high school students prepare for their state held exam - Matura. It has been downloaded by almost 4000 users since release. The app can be found HERE

Bosnian-Arabic Transliterator for Android

A quick project for personal use, I built an Android app that can transliterate text in Bosnian latin to Bosnian arabic and vice versa. It remains unpublished.

Red Cross of FBIH - First Aid App

Red Cross of FBIH - First Aid App is my first Kotlin project. It is a relatively simple android application including a Splash Screen, custom Array Adapters and Fragments. Its purpose is to show first aid procedures in various situations. It is currently work in progress

Web Development

Arebica Transliterator - Web Version

I decided to make a Web version of my Arebica transliterator app. It is a simple website with several features, including transliteration in both directions. It is online on GITHUB PAGES

Bosančica Transliterator

By slightly modifying the Arebica Website, I also created a transliterator for Bosnia's other unique script - Bosančica (also known as Bosnian Cyrillic). it is also online on GITHUB PAGES

Patisserie Website

I worked as the lead developer on creating a website for a fictional patisserie. It comprises of three separate pages and has many features. It is unpublished.
